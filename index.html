How to use this planner

Build your own ICRS 2026 schedule in a few taps 🐠
  1. 1
    Find talks. Use the day tabs to browse, or the search box to look up a title, author, or topic. Filters let you narrow by room, theme, or day.
  2. 2
    Star what you want. Tap the ☆ next to any talk to add it to your schedule — the star fills in and the count in My schedule goes up. Tap again to remove it.
  3. 3
    Tap a talk for details. Opening a talk shows its full abstract, room number, session, presenter, and co-authors — and you can star it from there too.
  4. 4
    Check My schedule. Your picks appear in time order for each day. If two overlap, you'll see a ⚠ clash badge — you can't be in both rooms at once.
  5. 5
    Jot down notes. Open any talk and scroll to My notes to record your thoughts. Tag talks as Revisit or Contact — the tags show on the talk list.

Handy extras

  • ↕️
    Sort and collapse. Use Sort to group the day by time, room, session or topic. Tap any session header to fold it away, or Collapse all to see just the headers — collapsed sessions still show how many talks you've picked or noted inside.
  • 📅
    Add to your calendar. In My schedule, Add to calendar (.ics) downloads your picks with times, rooms and your notes.
  • 📝
    Take notes. Add notes and Revisit / Contact tags in any talk. Notes stay on this device and disappear if you clear browser data. Export or back them up from My schedule.
  • 💾
    Back up your data. Backup saves picks and notes for every profile. Restore on the same site address (e.g. the GitHub Pages URL) to recover them on another device.
  • 📲
    Works offline. Add the page to your home screen and it keeps working with no wifi — handy in the session rooms.
  • 👤
    It's yours. Your name labels your schedule. Picks and notes stay on this device — they are lost if you clear browser data and do not move between devices unless you back up. Tap your name to switch profiles.
