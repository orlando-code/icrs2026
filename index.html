How to use this planner

Build your own ICRS 2026 schedule in a few taps 🐠
  1. 1
    Find talks. Use the day tabs to browse, or the search box to look up a title, author, or topic. Filters let you narrow by room, theme, or day.
  2. 2
    Star what you want. Tap the ☆ next to any talk to add it to your schedule — the star fills in and the count in My schedule goes up. Tap again to remove it.
  3. 3
    Tap a talk for details. Opening a talk shows its full abstract, room number, session, presenter, and co-authors — and you can star it from there too.
  4. 4
    Check My schedule. Your picks appear in time order for each day. If two overlap, you'll see a ⚠ clash badge — you can't be in both rooms at once.

Handy extras

  • 📅
    Add to your calendar. In My schedule, Add to calendar (.ics) downloads your talks with times and rooms for your phone or laptop calendar.
  • 🔗
    Use it on another device. Tap Copy share link and open that link on your phone — your picks come with it. Great for planning on a laptop, then using your phone at the venue.
  • 📲
    Works offline. Add the page to your home screen (your browser's Add to Home Screen) and it keeps working with no wifi — handy in the session rooms.
  • 👤
    It's yours. Your name in the top corner labels your schedule. Picks are saved on this device only and never leave it — updating the app won't erase them. Tap your name to switch or add a profile.
